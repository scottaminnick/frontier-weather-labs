⚠️ DISCLAIMER: NBM (National Blend of Models) guidance does not replace official NWS forecasts or Aviation products. Use at your own risk. Always consult official weather products for flight planning and operational decisions.

Data Values

  • % suffix = Probability/Percentage value
  • - = Missing or unavailable data
  • ± rows = Standard deviation (spread/uncertainty)

Temperature Colors

  • Blue = ≤32°F (freezing)
  • Red = ≥85°F (hot)

Flight Categories

  • VFR = Visual Flight Rules (best conditions)
  • MVFR = Marginal VFR
  • IFR = Instrument Flight Rules
  • LIFR = Low IFR (worst conditions)

Highlighted Values

  • Orange highlight = Significant value (≥50% probability, ≥20kt wind)

NBM Products

  • NBH = Hourly guidance out to 25 hours
  • NBS = 3-hourly guidance out to 72 hours
  • NBE = 12-hourly guidance out to ~8 days
  • NBX = 12-hourly guidance out to ~11 days
